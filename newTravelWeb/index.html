Best Places To Visit In Mongolia

On your way to explore the world, you can't miss the extra-ordinary beauty of Mongolia. With a spectacular vista of lands and mountains, this country welcomes you to enjoy the exciting silence of nature. Founded by Genghis Khan, Mongolia is a country with varying historic backgrounds and the influence of surrounding neighbors. The rugged landscape of the country inhabits a huge number of animals and a unique flora. You will not be sorry to visit this country. Let's begin our quest to some of the spectacular as well as popular places to visit in Mongolia.

Best Time To Visit Mongolia

In spring, the weather can be pretty harsh with strong winds and still cold temperatures. In June, summer is approaching and the temperature is between 20 and 30 degrees celsius. It still can be a bit chilly in the night but nothing compared to autumn. Because of the rain, the landscape is nice and green. If you want to visit the Gobi desert then come in May or September when it is not so hot. Winters are bitter cold but with the right equipment, there is also a lot to do in the Mongolian Winter.

Best Food To Eat In Mongolia

The most common rural dish is cooked mutton, usually without any other ingredients. To accompany the meats, vegetables and flour products may be used to create side dishes as well. In the city, every other local displays a sign saying "buuz". Those are steamed dumplings filled with meat. Other types of dumplings are boiled in water (bansh , manti), or deep fried in mutton fat (khuushuur). Other dishes combine the meat with rice or fresh noodles made into various stews (tsuivan , budaatai khuurga) or noodle soups (guriltai shöl). Sülen is a type of hot pot dish.
