Best Time To Visit Mongolia

In spring, the weather can be pretty harsh with strong winds and still cold temperatures. In June, summer is approaching and the temperature is between 20 and 30 degrees celsius. It still can be a bit chilly in the night but nothing compared to autumn. Because of the rain, the landscape is nice and green. If you want to visit the Gobi desert then come in May or September when it is not so hot. Winters are bitter cold but with the right equipment, there is also a lot to do in the Mongolian Winter.
